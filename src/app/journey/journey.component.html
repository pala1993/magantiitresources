President & CEO

Prasad Maganti is a President & CEO of Maganti IT Resources, LLC. With over 30+ years of experiences in IT industry, Prasad has accomplished executive with domestic and international experience in operations, P&L oversight, product development, marketing involving start-up, and growth organizations. Apart from IT business, he is a serial entrepreneur in other areas. He also serves as a Director of MBR Food Products, MBR Consumer Services, Maganti Oil Energy Inc, Maganti Group, LLC Maganti IT Solutions Pvt. Ltd. and Naugatuck Chamber Board of Director and Waterbury Chamber Member. He grew into this position by gaining experience in working with industry leaders from small, medium to large organizations where he served as a Senior IT Architect/Team Lead.

His result-oriented, decisive leader with proven success in new market identification and strategic positioning for information technology organizations has supported in business growth. With his high integrity he has always been an energetic leader known for ability to envision and create successful outcomes in complex situations. He has a diverse industry and functional expertise, with tenacious commitment to driving sales, profit and market-share growth. Prasad holds Master of Computer Science in Information Technology from Quinnipiac University, USA.
